Skin Analysis Using AI

Powering your skincare routine with artificial intelligence

How It Works

Derma Soul uses AI to analyze your uploaded skin photo and quiz responses to determine your skin type, skin tone, and acne severity. Personalized skincare advice is then provided to help you make informed decisions.

Skin Types

Normal Skin

Balanced, not too oily or dry. Few imperfections. Pores are not visible. Skin feels fresh and healthy.

Oily Skin

Shiny appearance, larger pores, and frequent breakouts. Needs oil control and lightweight skincare products.

Dry Skin

Feels tight, rough, or flaky. Needs deep hydration and gentle cleansers to maintain moisture levels.
